Julian Edwin 'Cannonball' Adderley

American Jazz Saxophonist

Here is some information about Cannonball Adderley:

  • Born in Tampa, Florida on September 15, 1928
  • Both his parents were teachers, and Cannonball was a high school band teacher for 2 years
  • "Cannonball" derives from "cannibal", which is what his colleagues called him because of his large apetitie
  • Cannonball's brother, Nat Adderley, played the trumpet and often played music with him

Here are some of Cannonball's greatest accomplishments:

  • Cannonball formed a group with his brother Nat and signed onto the Savoy jazz label in 1957
  • Miles Davis asked him to play with his group in 1957
  • Cannonball played with greats like John Coltrane, Art Blakey, Hank Jones, and Bill Evans
  • Some of his most famous albums he played on were Somethin' Else, Milestones, and Kind of Blue.
